01 / about

A bit about me

I'm a software engineer with about six years of experience building enterprise applications, handling production support, and coordinating across teams to keep critical systems running. Most recently I worked at Amazon, and before that at KFin Technologies in India, where I worked on financial systems serving mutual fund investors and asset management companies.

My core stack is Java and Spring Boot on the backend, Angular and TypeScript on the frontend, with SQL databases and AWS underneath. I've spent a lot of time in the part of engineering that does not always get attention — debugging production issues, hardening workflows, and making sure the system behaves correctly when real users do unexpected things.

I'm currently pursuing an MBA alongside my engineering work, with a long-term goal of growing into an engineering management role. I care about writing software that is dependable and about helping the people around me do their best work.
